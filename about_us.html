Overview

SP Seva Sansthan Foundation (SPSS Foundation) is a dynamic social organization committed to uplifting underprivileged communities, emphasizing education, health, environment, and women's empowerment. Established in 2021, Our mission is to address underprivileged communities' challenges through local insights and strong community support.
Specializing in grassroots-level interventions, we have Actively engaged in critical areas like cancer and menstrual hygiene. We aim to make a significant impact across sectors, serving as a catalyst for justice, dignity and to bring sustainable change in the lives of underprivileged individuals and communities.
Our Vision
To work as a catalyst in bringing sustainable change in the life of underprivileged children, youth and women, with a life cycle approach of development.
Our Mission
To alleviate the problems of the underprivileged & their struggles to obtain a life of justice and dignity.
Our Values
  • Transparency: We ensure complete transparency in all our efforts and workings with Internal as well as external stakeholders of the organisation.
  • Respect: We ensure respect towards each individual and their views & feelings inside and outside the organisation, despite its cast, creed, gender or colour.
  • Integrity: We work with integrity and ensure the dignity of all those involved in our endeavours To comply with all laws, rules and regulations bestowed upon us as part of an organisation.
  • Empathy: To be empathetic to all those associated with SPSS Foundation and ensure a high sense of understanding of the feelings of all our stakeholders.
Our Goals
  • We empower Youth through encouragement, education and employment to build a strong and prosperous society.
  • Work on various aspects impacting children to build an empowered future generation.
  • Empowering Citizens to live with dignity and get equal rights for them in society.
  • Creating a healthy, sustainable and Safe environment for citizens to live.

The Founder’s Message

“Our long-term efforts at SPSS Foundation towards development, our commitment to include underprivileged children and women in mainstream development continues to bring positive changes in their lives. When we review our work, we feel inspired that we have made an impactful contribution to achieving these sustainable goals. Recognizing the reciprocal relationship between the efforts of grassroots voluntary organizations like ours and to achieve our goals reveals the critical role our organization plays and establishes the fact that, both small and large efforts toward development are required to change lives.”

- Jigar Vaishnav | Founder & Executive Director
