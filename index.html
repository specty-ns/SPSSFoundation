Welcome to SP Seva Sansthan Foundation

At SP Seva Sansthan Foundation, we are dedicated to making a positive impact by uplifting lives and creating a lasting change in communities. Our foundation is built on a commitment to social welfare, education, health and community development. with a vision for a brighter future, we work tirelessly to address pressing social issues and empower individuals to lead fulfilling lives.

Our Focus Area

Education
Supporting education of children & providing vocational training to young adults.
Environment
Initiatives on waste management, tree planting, recycling & other sustainable practices.
Community Prosperity
Empowering rural futures through entrepreneurship, upskilling, sustainable living & holistic development initiatives.
Health
Organizing medical camps, outreach programs & menstrual health initiatives, etc.
Women Empowerment
Empowering women with education & supporting their micro-enterprises, etc.
Through these diverse range of activities, SP Seva Sansthan Foundation strives to bring about social welfare, create awareness, and drive positive social change in the society. The Foundation's efforts are aimed at uplifting underprivileged individuals, protecting the environment, empowering communities, and the welfare of animals.

Our Impact Is Changing Lives

Saved 1002 lives through timely emergency ambulance services.
Provided healthcare services to 10,954 individuals via 46 free health camps.
Quality education to 750 children through 9 Kids Learning Centre's.
Empowered 308 beneficiaries with essential digital skills
Guided 1902 students toward informed academic and career decisions.
Planted 1811 trees and conducted 7 cleanliness drives to promote sustainability and improve community hygiene. conservation initiative.
Reached 51,407 individuals through essentials distribution, COVID-19 relief, and awareness workshops on vital social issues.
Impacted 24,809 individuals by conducting 332 awareness workshops on menstrual health.
Trained 50 women in vocational skills and held 4 workshops benefitting 117 women on gender equality and entrepreneurship.
Cervical Cancer: Prevention Through Screening at PHCs trained 326 ASHA workers and 39 staff nurses to enhance awareness and screening efforts. Reached 55,773 individuals through door-to-door campaigns and 2775 women via 57 awareness workshops,screening 2943 women in 47 camps.
Breast Cancer Awareness and Screening conducted 11 screening camps, screening 240 women for early detection of breast cancer. Organized 11 awareness workshops, educating 1000 women on breast cancer prevention and self-examination.
