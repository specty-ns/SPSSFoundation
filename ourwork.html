Welcome to SP Seva Sansthan Foundation

At SP Seva Sansthan Foundation, we are dedicated to making a positive impact by uplifting lives and creating a lasting change in communities. Our foundation is built on a commitment to social welfare, education, health and community development. with a vision for a brighter future, we work tirelessly to address pressing social issues and empower individuals to lead fulfilling lives.

Our Focus Area

Education
Supporting education of children & providing vocational training to young adults.
Environment
Initiatives on waste management, tree planting, recycling & other sustainable practices.
Community Prosperity
Empowering rural futures through entrepreneurship, upskilling, sustainable living & holistic development initiatives.
Health
Organizing medical camps, outreach programs & menstrual health initiatives, etc.
Women Empowerment
Empowering women with education & supporting their micro-enterprises, etc.
Through these diverse range of activities, SP Seva Sansthan Foundation strives to bring about social welfare, create awareness, and drive positive social change in the society. The Foundation's efforts are aimed at uplifting underprivileged individuals, protecting the environment, empowering communities, and the welfare of animals.
