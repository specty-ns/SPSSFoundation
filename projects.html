Ongoing Project

Planted 1,811 trees and conducted 7 cleanliness drives to promote sustainability and improve community hygiene. conservation initiative.
Organized 2 free health camps , offering health check-ups to a total of 413 individuals.
Quality education to 750 children through 9 Kids Learning Centre's.
Empowered 308 beneficiaries with essential digital skills
Guided 2,158 students towards informed academic and career decisions.
Through Mission Unnati , we reached and supported 23,071 beneficiaries via distribution drives focused on uplifting communities.
Impacted 24,809 individuals by conducting 332 awareness workshops on menstrual health.
Trained 50 women in vocational skills and held 4 workshops benefitting 117 women on gender equality and entrepreneurship.
Cervical Cancer: Prevention Through Screening at PHCs trained 326 ASHA workers and 39 staff nurses to enhance awareness and screening efforts. Reached 55,773 individuals through door-to-door campaigns and 2,775 women via 57 awareness workshops,screening 2,943 women in 47 camps.
Volunteer-driven initiatives addressed key social issues, engaging communities and benefiting 375 individuals.

Completed Project

Saved 1,002 lives through timely emergency ambulance services.
Provided healthcare services to 10,954 individuals via 46 free health camps.
Breast Cancer Awareness and Screening conducted 11 screening camps, screening 240 women for early detection of breast cancer. Organized 11 awareness workshops, educating 1,000 women on breast cancer prevention and self-examination.

Covid-19 Project

Reached 51,407 individuals through essentials distribution, COVID-19 relief, and awareness workshops on vital social issues.
