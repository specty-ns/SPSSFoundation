Volunteers & Interns

We believe that unless members of civil society are involved proactively in the process of development, sustainable change will not happen. We encourage and invite Volunteers & Interns to be an active part of our organization and share the same vision and purpose as us – to work for the welfare of children and their families. Volunteers & Interns are the backbone of every organization – they not only carry the organization’s ideals within them but also spread the message far and beyond, sensitizing society towards the cause. We will mentor and support you in whatever role you choose to do.
If you have time to give and the commitment to do something for society, we are keen to welcome you to our team. Fill out the volunteer & Internship form and get in touch with us.

Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR)

We believe that sustainable development is a shared responsibility. By working together with forward-thinking corporates, we can create a more equitable and empowered society, where communities thrive. We invite organizations to partner with us through their Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) initiatives to bring about lasting social, environmental, and economic change.

Why Partner with Us?

SPSS Foundation is a trusted partner for corporate organizations looking to make a significant impact through their CSR funding. Here’s why you should collaborate with us:
  1. Measurable Impact: We ensure that all CSR projects are driven by measurable outcomes, using data to track progress and maximize the impact of your investment.
  2. Sustainable Solutions: The foundation is dedicated to offering sustainable solutions to societal challenges, steering away from short-term fixes to address underlying issues effectively.
  3. Transparency and Accountability: We uphold the highest standards of transparency by regularly sharing updates, financial reports, and progress on funded projects, ensuring that your contributions are used effectively.
  4. Holistic Approach: Our projects address a wide range of community needs, from education and healthcare to environmental conservation and women empowerment, ensuring comprehensive and sustainable development.
  5. Commitment to Innovation: SPSS Foundation continuously explores innovative solutions and utilizes technology to address societal challenges and create positive change.
  6. Strong Partnerships: We have developed robust partnerships with various organizations, government bodies, and local stakeholders, allowing us to scale our initiatives for maximum impact.
  7. Cost-Effective: With a focus on efficient use of resources, we ensure that your CSR contributions achieve the greatest possible impact with optimal resource utilization.

Key Areas for CSR Collaboration

SPSS Foundation offers a range of impactful projects aligned with multiple Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), where corporate partners can contribute their CSR funds. Some of the key areas for collaboration include:
  1. Education and Skill Development: Support our Kids Learning Centers and Digital Literacy Initiatives that provide quality education and skill development opportunities for underprivileged children and youth.
  2. Healthcare and Well-being: Contribute to medical camps, health awareness programs, and initiatives like the Aayushman Aadhar project that provide emergency healthcare services and health support to marginalized communities.
  3. Women Empowerment: Partner with us to promote gender equality and support women’s socio-economic development through education, skills training, and entrepreneurship support.
  4. Environmental Conservation: Join us in our environmental initiatives, including tree-planting campaigns, waste management programs, and sustainable agriculture projects that address climate change and protect biodiversity.
  5. Livelihood Support: Through our livelihood programs and microfinance initiatives, help empower individuals with the resources and training needed to improve their economic conditions.

Our Commitment to the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs)

SPSS Foundation aligns its efforts with several United Nations SDGs to ensure that our initiatives contribute to global sustainable development. Your CSR funds can help us further these goals:
  1. SDG 1: No Poverty: Reducing poverty through livelihood programs and skill development to improve economic conditions in communities.
  2. SDG 2: Zero Hunger: Supporting agricultural initiatives and food security programs to achieve zero hunger.
  3. SDG 3: Good Health and Well-being: Providing healthcare services, medical camps, and emergency care to improve health outcomes for underprivileged communities.
  4. SDG 4: Quality Education: Ensuring access to quality education and learning opportunities for underprivileged children through our Kids Learning Centers and skill development programs.
  5. SDG 5: Gender Equality: Empowering women through education, skills training, and socio-economic support to promote gender equality
  6. SDG 6: Decent Work and Economic Growth: Promoting economic growth and job creation through livelihood and entrepreneurship programs.
  7. SDG 7: Reduced Inequality: Working to reduce inequalities within communities through inclusive development and equal opportunities for all.
  8. SDG 8: Sustainable Cities and Communities: Contributing to the creation of resilient, sustainable communities through rural development, infrastructure projects, and climate action.
  9. SDG 9: Responsible Consumption and Production: Encouraging sustainable practices and responsible consumption through environmental conservation efforts, such as tree planting and waste management.
  10. SDG 10: Climate Action: Addressing climate change through initiatives that promote environmental sustainability and support eco-friendly practices.
  11. SDG 11: Life on Land: Protecting terrestrial ecosystems and biodiversity through tree-planting campaigns and environmental conservation.
  12. SDG 12: Partnerships for the Goals: Collaborating with corporates, government bodies, and local organizations to strengthen partnerships and achieve sustainable development goals together.

How Your Organization Can Get Involved?

  1. Direct Donations: Contribute directly to any of our ongoing projects that align with your corporate values. Your financial support can drive tangible change in the communities we serve.
  2. Employee Engagement Programs We offer employee engagement initiatives, such as volunteering opportunities, skill-based volunteering, and corporate donations matched with employee contributions, to foster a deeper connection to our cause.
  3. Project Sponsorships: Corporates can sponsor specific programs or projects, allowing you to direct your CSR funds to areas most relevant to your corporate social responsibility goals.
  4. Partnerships and Collaborations: Explore strategic partnerships to co-create impactful programs that align with both your corporate values and our mission of empowering communities.

Our Commitment to Your CSR Goals

By partnering with SP Seva Sansthan Foundation, your organization can demonstrate its commitment to creating a positive societal impact. We offer detailed impact reports, ensuring that your CSR funds are invested responsibly and effectively, generating lasting change.
